How It Works 使用方法

From finding your text to writing your essay 从找到文本到写出文章

1

Find Your Text 找到您的文本

Select the book your child is studying. Each guide is built specifically for that text. 选择孩子正在学习的书籍。每份指南都专门为该文本设计。

2

Work Through the Guide 学习指南

Follow the 5-step analysis. See what sophisticated reading looks like for this book. 按照5步分析法学习。了解这本书的高水平阅读分析。

Get Support 获得支持
