## Technical Notes for AI Voice Generation
*   **Tone**: Calm, authoritative, analytical. Like a detective explaining a clue.
*   **Pacing**: Leave space for the animations to draw (approx 1-2 seconds) at the cues.
*   **Emphasis**: Emphasize words like "Data", "Hook", "Frightened".
