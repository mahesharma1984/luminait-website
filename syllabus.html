10-Week Curriculum

10周课程大纲

Analytical Writing for Secondary Students 中学生分析性写作课程

Our structured 10-week course takes students from foundational reading skills to writing complete analytical essays. By the end, students will be able to identify literary devices, explain their significance, and construct cohesive arguments — skills that transfer to NAPLAN, VCE, IB, and beyond.

我们精心设计的10周课程，带领学生从基础阅读技能逐步进阶到完成完整的分析性文章。课程结束时，学生将能够识别文学手法、阐释其意义并构建连贯的论证——这些技能适用于NAPLAN、VCE、IB等各类考试。

Three Phases of Learning 三个学习阶段

Pre-Analysis 分析准备阶段

Context & Pattern

背景与模式

Students build foundational understanding of the text: genre conventions, reading strategies, character relationships, and how the text creates meaning.

学生建立对文本的基础理解：文体规范、阅读策略、人物关系，以及文本如何构建意义。

Weeks 0 – 0.5 第0周 – 第0.5周
Analysis 分析阶段

Device Analysis

手法分析

Students learn to identify and analyze literary devices through the stages of Freytag's Pyramid: exposition, rising action, climax, falling action, and resolution.

学生通过弗莱塔格金字塔的各个阶段学习识别和分析文学手法：开端、上升、高潮、下降和结局。

Weeks 1 – 5 第1周 – 第5周
Synthesis 综合阶段

Thesis & Essay

论点与文章

Students synthesize their analysis into thesis statements and complete essays, learning paragraph structure and argument development.

学生将分析综合成论点陈述和完整文章，学习段落结构和论证展开。

Weeks 6 – 10 第6周 – 第10周

Key Frameworks 核心框架

TVODE
Topic, Voice, Object, Detail, Effect 主题、声音、对象、细节、效果

A sentence-level structure for building analytical statements that connect textual evidence to interpretation. 句子层级的分析结构，将文本证据与解读相连接。

TEEL
Topic, Explanation, Evidence, Link 主题、解释、证据、联结

A paragraph-level structure for organising analytical essays with clear topic sentences and supporting evidence. 段落层级的结构，用于组织带有清晰主题句和支撑证据的分析文章。

DCCEPS
Reasoning Progression 推理进阶

A framework for developing reasoning skills from description through to evaluation and synthesis. 从描述到评价与综合的推理能力发展框架。
