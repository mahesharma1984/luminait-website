The 10-Week Plan

Representative progression used across texts with local adaptation by chapter and assessment schedule.

Weeks 1–2

Foundation: context, core pattern, vocabulary control, annotation baseline.

Weeks 3–5

Analysis: device selection, evidence ranking, line-level reasoning.

Weeks 6–8

Writing: paragraph planning, TVODE execution, feedback cycles.

Weeks 9–10

Completion: timed response structure, exam transfer, final benchmark.
