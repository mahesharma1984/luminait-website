Week 4 — Narrative Voice 第4周 — 叙述声音

Each week, students complete a structured worksheet that guides them through device analysis. This sample from Week 4 shows how students learn to identify narrative voice devices and explain their significance to the text's overall meaning.

每周，学生完成一份结构化的练习，引导他们进行文学手法分析。这份第4周的样例展示了学生如何学习识别叙述声音手法，并解释其对文本整体意义的重要性。

Worksheet Structure 练习结构

1 Thesis Alignment 论点对齐 Pages 6-7 第6-7页

Shows how this week's focus connects to the overall meaning of the text. Students see where their analysis fits in the bigger picture — building toward a cohesive essay thesis.

展示本周重点如何与文本整体意义相连接。学生能看到他们的分析如何融入更大的图景——逐步构建一个连贯的文章论点。

Example 示例

"This week we examine narrative voice in Chapters 15-21. Scout's evolving perspective reveals how empathy develops through direct experience with injustice."

"本周我们分析第15-21章中的叙述声音。斯库特不断演变的视角揭示了共情如何通过直接经历不公正而发展。"

2 Comprehension Check 理解检查 Page 8 第8页

Quick entry activity ensuring students have surface-level understanding before moving to deeper analysis. Checks character motivations, key events, and basic plot comprehension.

快速入门活动，确保学生在进入深度分析之前具有表层理解。检查人物动机、关键事件和基本情节理解。

3 Device Analysis 手法分析 Pages 9-18 第9-18页

The core of each worksheet: a 6-step scaffolded process for analyzing 2-4 literary devices. Students move from identification through to explaining effect and significance.

每份练习的核心：一个6步骤的脚手架流程，用于分析2-4个文学手法。学生从识别逐步过渡到解释效果和意义。

Example Device: Dramatic Irony 示例手法：戏剧性反讽

Students identify dramatic irony in Scout's narration, analyze what readers understand that Scout doesn't, and explain how this creates empathy for the trial's injustice.

学生在斯库特的叙述中识别戏剧性反讽，分析读者理解而斯库特不理解的内容，并解释这如何为审判的不公正创造共情。

4 TVODE Connection TVODE连接 Page 19 第19页

Students synthesize their device analysis into TVODE sentences — the building blocks for analytical paragraphs. This connects individual insights to the essay-writing skills developed in later weeks.

学生将他们的手法分析综合成TVODE句子——分析段落的构建模块。这将个别见解与后续几周发展的文章写作技能相连接。

Worksheets Are Tiered 练习分层设计

Every worksheet comes in three versions to match student ability levels: 每份练习有三个版本，以匹配学生的能力水平：

Beginner 初级
  • 2 devices per worksheet
  • 每份练习2个手法
  • 4-step simplified process
  • 4步简化流程
  • Sentence frames provided
  • 提供句子框架
  • More guided prompts
  • 更多引导性提示
Standard 标准
  • 3-4 devices per worksheet
  • 每份练习3-4个手法
  • Full 6-step process
  • 完整6步流程
  • Guided response structure
  • 引导性回答结构
  • Some independent work
  • 部分独立完成
Advanced 高级
  • 4-5 devices per worksheet
  • 每份练习4-5个手法
  • 6-step + extension tasks
  • 6步流程+拓展任务
  • Open response format
  • 开放式回答格式
  • Critical evaluation required
  • 需要批判性评价
