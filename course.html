Common questions

What if my child's text isn't listed?
We can teach the method on any text your child is studying at school. Just let us know what they're reading and we'll prepare materials for that text.
Is this tutoring?
It's more structured than traditional tutoring. Your child follows a curriculum with specific weekly outputs, not open-ended sessions. Every week has a clear goal and measurable outcome.
What if they fall behind?
The weekly structure keeps students on track. If they need extra time on a concept, we can adjust. The progress scores will show where they need more support.
Can I see their work during the course?
Yes. You'll receive weekly reports showing their score and what they worked on. The end-of-course progress report gives you the full picture.
What year levels is this for?
Years 10-12, with a focus on VCE English Literature. The method works for any analytical writing at secondary level.
When do cohorts start?
New cohorts start at the beginning of each school term. Contact us to join the next available group.
